Privacy Policy

Privacy‑first by design: our processing is client‑side whenever technically possible.

1) Local Processing

Your files are processed locally in your browser and are not sent to our servers.

2) Analytics

We may use privacy‑friendly, cookie‑free analytics to improve the site. No personal file data is collected.

3) Cookies

We avoid cookies unless essential for functionality. If used, we will clearly indicate why.

4) Data Retention

Because files stay on your device, we do not retain your documents on our servers.

5) Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact link on the home page.
