Drag & drop images here (JPG/PNG/WEBP/AVIF/HEIC)
All processing happens locally in your browser.
Note: PDF can embed JPEG & PNG. We will convert WEBP/AVIF/HEIC to JPEG by default (smaller file). For PNG, you can choose lossless embedding to preserve transparency.
Tip: Reorder images below by dragging the handles.

How it works

  • Everything is client-side using pdf-lib + Canvas.
  • HEIC/HEIF decoded in-browser via a small WASM helper (no upload).
  • AVIF uses the browser decoder (Chrome/Edge ok; some older browsers may not support).
Privacy: no uploads. Everything stays on your device.
